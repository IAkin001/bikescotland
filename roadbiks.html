What Are Road Bikes?

Road bikes are designed for efficient and fast riding on paved surfaces, like smooth roads and bike paths. They typically have narrow, high-pressure tires and drop handlebars, placing the rider in a position that optimizes aerodynamics and speed. Road bikes come in various types, including race bikes, aero bikes, endurance bikes, and climbing bikes, each with specific features and geometries for different riding purposes
